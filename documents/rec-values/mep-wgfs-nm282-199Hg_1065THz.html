The International System of Units

199Hg+ ion, 5 d 10 6 s   2 S 1 / 2 − 5 d 9 6 s 2   2 D 5 / 2 unperturbed optical transition

1.  Recommended value [1] of the frequency:

f199Hg+=1 064 721 609 899 145.30⁢Hz

equivalent to

λ199Hg+=281 568 867.591 968 5⁢fm

with a relative uncertainty of 1.9 × 10 − 15 .

This radiation was already endorsed as a secondary representation of the definition of the second.

2.  Source data

Adopted value

f199Hg+=1 064 721 609 899 145.30⁢Hz
u c / y = 1.9 × 10 − 15

calculated from

f199Hg+/Hz u/Hz source data
1 064 721 609 899 145.30 0.69 [2]

As this value was issued from only one laboratory the CCTF considered it prudent to estimate a relative standard uncertainty of 1.9 × 10 − 15 .
