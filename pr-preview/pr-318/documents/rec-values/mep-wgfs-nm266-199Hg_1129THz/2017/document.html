1.  Recommended value [1] of the frequency in the CIPM List of Frequencies [2]

f ( 199 Hg ) = 1 128 575 290 808 154.4 ⁢ Hz

equivalent to

λ ( 199 Hg ) = 265 637 977.759 838 7 ⁢ fm ,

with a relative standard uncertainty of 5 × 10 − 16 .

This radiation was endorsed by the CCTF [1] as a secondary representation of the definition of the second.

2.  Source data

The adopted value of f ( 199 H g ) = 1 128 575 290 808 154.4 ⁢ Hz with a relative standard uncertainty of 5 × 10 − 16 has been calculated by the methods presented in [3], [4], [5], [6], with the data from the table of evaluated frequencies (2017) attached below. The estimated relative standard uncertainty of 5 × 10 − 16 takes into account the relative uncertainty of the 87Sr value of 4 × 10 − 16 governing the evaluation procedure and quantitatively unknown contributions of the correlations between the data of [8].
