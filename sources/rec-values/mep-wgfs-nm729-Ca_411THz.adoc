== Calcium 40 Ion (stem:[f ~~ 411" "rm(THz)])

*^40^Ca^+^, 4s ^2^S~1/2~ – 3d ^2^D~5/2~ unperturbed optical transition*

=== Recommended value <<cctf21>> of the frequency in the CIPM List of Frequencies <<ci2006>>

stem:[f](^40^Ca^+^) stem:[= 411042129776399.8 " "rm(Hz)]

equivalent to

stem:[ii(lambda)](^40^Ca^+^) = stem:[729347276.793944 " "rm(fm)],

with a relative standard uncertainty of stem:[2.4 xx 10^(−15)].

=== Source data

The adopted value of stem:[f](^40^Ca^+^) = stem:[411042129776399.8 " "rm(Hz)] with a relative standard uncertainty of stem:[2.4 xx 10^(−15)]

has been calculated by the methods presented in <<margolis>>, <<robertsson>>, <<oates>>, <<riehle>>, with the data from the table of frequencies (2017) attached below. Since the frequency and the associated uncertainty of ref. <<chwalla>> is not compatible with the other data, the uncertainty given in the original publication was increased to 3 Hz to make it statistically more consistent. This value has very little effect, and so the recommended frequency value comes essentially only from measurements made by two labs. Thus, the final uncertainty has been increased by a factor of two following the procedures outlined in ref. <<riehle>>.
